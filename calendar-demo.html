Event Calendar

Stay up to date with upcoming maintenance schedules, webinars, and valve training sessions. This is a live demo using a public US Holidays calendar.

How to Customize

To use your own calendar:

  1. Go to your Google Calendar settings on desktop.
  2. Select the calendar you want to share from the left sidebar.
  3. Scroll down to "Integrate calendar".
  4. Copy the "Embed code" provided by Google.
  5. Replace the <iframe> tag in this file with your copied code.
