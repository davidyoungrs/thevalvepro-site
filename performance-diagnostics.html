Core Capabilities

Intelligent Capabilities for
Critical Applications

Four pillars of advanced diagnostics, engineered for the realities of process-critical control valves.

Real-Time Asset Health Index (AHI)

Stop guessing. Every valve in your fleet is continuously assigned a dynamic health score (0–100%). We aggregate command signals, actual position, process variables, and actuator pressure to give you an instant, plant-wide view of asset integrity.

Advanced Predictive Diagnostics

Decades of hands-on failure analysis, codified into ML models. Automatically detect early onset of mechanical degradation — from stiction, hysteresis, and deadband issues to pneumatic leaks and internal cavitation — long before they trigger a process alarm.

Prescriptive, Actionable Alerts

Data without context creates noise. When a fault is predicted, TheValve.app provides specific, engineering-backed recommendations — not just an alert, but a directive: "85% probability of stiction in V-104. Schedule packing adjustment during the next micro-stop."

Digital Twin & Performance Curves

Visually benchmark a valve's live, real-world performance against its OEM factory-spec design curves. Instantly recognise deviations and validate repair effectiveness — without taking the asset offline.
