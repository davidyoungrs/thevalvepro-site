About

Technical Expertise Backed by Global Leadership

With a Master’s degree in Mechanical Engineering and more than three decades of hands‑on and strategic experience, David Young brings a unique blend of technical authority and executive leadership to the global valve industry. His career spans major engineering and service organisations including Severn, Petrofac, and Celeros Flow Technology.

David has led multi‑country service operations, engineered complex valve and rotating equipment solutions, and delivered commercial growth through innovative service models, preventative maintenance strategies, and digital performance tools. His background combines deep engineering pedigree with proven business acumen, making him a trusted partner for owners and end users
