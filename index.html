About

Technical Expertise Backed by Global Leadership

David combines 30+ years of hands-on engineering with high-level strategic leadership. His approach is grounded in the reality of field operations but driven by commercial and safety objectives.

Early Career & Foundations
Starting as an apprentice at ClydeUnion Pumps, David built a rock-solid foundation in rotating equipment and valve engineering. This "shop floor to boardroom" journey ensures that his strategic advice is always technically sound and practically executable.
Leadership Philosophy
Managing global teams at Celeros and Severn taught David that true reliability comes from people as much as process. He focuses on building competence, streamlining handover continuity, and fostering a culture of ownership within engineering teams.
Strategic Impact
Focused on the intersection of technical integrity and commercial viability. Whether it's extending asset life or optimizing inventory for a multi-million dollar plant, the goal is always measurable value and reduced risk.
